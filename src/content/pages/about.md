## Philosophy

**"Start from the ground up."**

I believe the best systems are:
- **Simple**: Easy to understand and modify
- **Observable**: You can see what's happening
- **Resilient**: They degrade gracefully, not catastrophically
- **Measured**: Data-driven, not opinionated

I write about what I’m learning, what breaks, and what actually helps build better judgment.
