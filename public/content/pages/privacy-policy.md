# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: April 16, 2026**

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how I collect, use, and protect your information when you visit this website. I am committed to respecting your privacy and protecting your personal data.

## Information I Collect

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Usage data**: Pages visited, time spent, referring URL
- **Device information**: Browser type, operating system, IP address (anonymized)
- **Analytics**: Via Google Analytics 4 (GA4)

### Information You Provide
- **Contact form submissions**: Name, email, message content
- **Newsletter signups**: Email address
- **Comments or feedback**: Any information you voluntarily submit

### Cookies and Tracking
I use:
- **Analytics cookies**: To understand how you use the site
- **Functional cookies**: To remember your preferences
- **No third-party advertisement cookies** (unless you opt-in)

## How I Use Your Information

1. **Improve the website**: Analytics help me understand what content is valuable
2. **Respond to communications**: I'll use your contact info to reply to messages
3. **Send newsletters**: Only if you explicitly subscribe
4. **Comply with legal obligations**: If required by law enforcement

I do **not** sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties without your consent.

## Third-Party Services

This website uses:
- **Google Analytics 4**: For usage analytics. See [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- **Vercel**: For hosting. See [Vercel's Privacy Policy](https://vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy)

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: Request what data I hold about you
- **Correction**: Request corrections to inaccurate data
- **Deletion**: Request that I delete your data
- **Opt-out**: Disable analytics or unsubscribe from emails

To exercise these rights, contact me at: [contact@biratcodes.dev]

## Data Retention

- **Contact form data**: Retained for 1 year
- **Newsletter list**: Until you unsubscribe
- **Analytics data**: 14 months (GA4 default)

## Security

I implement reasonable technical measures to protect your data, including:
- HTTPS encryption for all connections
- Secure hosting on Vercel
- No storage of sensitive information (passwords, etc.)

## Children's Privacy

This website is not directed to children under 13. If I learn that a child under 13 has provided personal information, I will delete it immediately.

## Changes to This Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. I will notify you of material changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
